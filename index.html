About Jorge

Origami isn't just about folding paper - it's about unfolding your mind. Through each careful crease and deliberate fold, we find moments of peace and clarity. In our fast-paced world, this ancient art offers a path to mindfulness and relaxation.

Through workshops and demonstrations, I share my passion for origami with others, helping them discover the meditative and rewarding nature of this ancient art form.
